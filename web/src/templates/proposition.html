Our Key Value Propositions

We recognize the current market is blown out of proportion for everyday people due to their high costs and lack of innovation, as well as simplicity. We would like to tackle this head on.

Our desktop humanoid robot helps, primarily work-at-home adults, and all ages of people who want a social and learning experience by avoiding costly and complicated robots on the market and opting for an easy-to-use/build interaction toy that can promote learning and daily satisfaction via conversation, unlike Moxie and Milko 3 which are expensive. We believe our customers will build a connection with their BotBuddy by having to build their robot in a simple manner, and also have customability features after being completed. Most toys of this manner typically become stale, and won't be used over a period of time, thus fading out; we would like to keep the user interested with the BotBuddy via add ons and other features that continue to keep the custopmer happy. We were formally names TheraBot, but needed to generalize because it came off too therapuerdic.

Notable Features:

  • Affordable
  • Emotion responses
  • Customability
  • Portabaility
  • Multiple Languages
  • Fun toy for all ages
