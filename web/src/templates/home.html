BotBuddy

A small desk robot for ECE140b project. Designed to be a companion robot friend - we intend to simulate emotional features like sassiness, happiness, saddness, and other typical friend-like emotions. We envision that this product will be successfull in that it can entertain the user, and be of aid to relieve stress momentarily, as working from home becomes more and more common. This can be a modern day toy for any age group.
